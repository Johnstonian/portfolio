About

I build websites.

I'm a front-end developer who loves the web. I'm passionate about semantic and standards based development. Efficient code, creative user experiences, robust typography, CSS3, and the semantic web are just a few of my interests. I'm excited about the future of the web and strive to make it a better experience every day.
