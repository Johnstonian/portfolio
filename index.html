About

I build websites.

I'm a front-end developer who loves the web. I'm passionate about semantic and standards based development. Efficient code, creative user experiences, robust typography, CSS3, and the semantic web are just a few of my interests. I'm excited about the future of the web and strive to make it a better experience every day.

Like many, I got started with web development in the world of Geocities and Angelfire. My latest work has expanded into the modern web landscape of robustly featured internet applications.

In addition to my passion for the web, I also enjoy reading fiction, training for adventure runs, listening to music, playing soccer, and doing my best to keep the 80’s alive through my son and daughter by forcing pop culture references of the era upon them.

I currently work at COUNTRY Financial where he develops dynamic applications for the web using PHP, MySQL, JavaScript, CSS3, and HTML5.

If you’d like to get in touch with me about freelance projects, please email ryanjohnston at gmail dot com.
